{% translate 'Our History' %}

{% blocktrans %} Pl4n3t is a brand that emerged in 2021 as a result of tax reforms that opened an opportunity to generate income in the environmental area. It was then when environmental projects began to emerge in collaboration with international organizations, focused on the quantification of removals, specifically carbon sequestration. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} With the objective of the need to comply with the agreements of the Montreal Treaty and the Colombian and international regulations, which require the quantification of emissions and compensation through verified carbon certificates. To expand its market, Amanecer en el palmar through the Pl4n3t brand decided to participate in 2022, in an innovation project called "SENAInnova Productividad para las Empresas 2.0" supported by SENAInnova - Productividad para las empresas which is a project between SENA and Colombia Productiva, which is an entity of the Ministry of Industry and Tourism. The objective was to formulate a project to quantify the greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions of companies. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} In the year 2022, the company Amanecer en el Palmar under the brand Pl4n3t began to offer and perform greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions quantification services to companies, events, organizations and products. Finally in mid-November 2022, Amanecer en el Palmar were selected in the innovation project, which led to the creation of a technological platform for quantifying the corporate footprint (Carbon, Plastic and Hydric). {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} In summary, Pl4n3t is a brand that was born from the company Amanecer en el Palmar, with the objective of taking advantage of the opportunities of the environmental market, with a focus on the quantification of GHG emissions. Its collaboration with Amanecer en el Palmar and its technological platform demonstrate its commitment to sustainability and environmental care. {% endblocktrans %}
